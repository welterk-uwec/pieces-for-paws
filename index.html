About Us

Pieces for Paws Art Gallery and Animal Rescue is a non-profit organization that shows and sells artwork to fund animal rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption into a new home. The organization strives to help animals of the Eau Claire County area and greater with hopes of them being adopted into a new home.
