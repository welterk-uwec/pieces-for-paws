About Us

Pieces for Paws Art Gallery and Animal Rescue is a non-profit organization that shows and sells artwork to fund animal rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption into a new home. The organization strives to help animals of the Eau Claire County area and greater with hopes of them being adopted into a new home.

Our Art Center

Local artists can have their pieces shown in the art gallery in downtown Eau Claire where they can choose to sell either to fully support the animal rescue, or partially, through a percentage of the price. Customers can buy art, donate, contribute through community events, or help give pets new homes!
